ThePeace

Share short video messages

ThePeace is an app that makes it easy and delightful to share the moments of everyday through short video messages. I was thrilled when Contafe Lab approached me to help design their app. We collaborated on the user flow and design vision, creating a simple app to share and create video messages. When they told me their vision of the app—to spread peace in the world through giving and receiving peaceful hugs—I of course wanted to get involved. The purpose of ThePeace application is to help people feel close no matter the distance, enabling them to stay connected in meaningful ways. Just keep clicking get a hug button and get hugs from different people in the world.

THE STYLE

ThePeace uses a bright but slightly muted color palette. I researched other apps that focused on spreading goodwill, and discovered that a slightly muted but diverse palette with blue as the core color seemed to be a common design pattern. Based on these results, I created a palette that appears lighthearted and contrasting, but not overly bright or too bold.

THE MARK

The logo began as a brainstorming session to review what design elements evoke the sense of peace and goodwill. Focusing on these keywords, I developed an illustration of a dove as a simple and elegant mark that is legible in small sizes. Since I anticipated that the logo would need to work across different screen locations, such as in the navigation bar, on a loading screen or on a marketing website, I used the high contrast color palette to show the dove's features.

THE PROTOTYPE

Although the app is a simple interface, we decided that in order to ensure success, that we would include a few screens to give the user context for the app. Users can swipe through or read the directions on how to send and receive messages.

I also designed the landing page for users to get updates on the app and directions on where to download the latest version. The app is currently available to download in the Google Play store.
