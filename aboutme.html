About Me

Kelly was born in Paris, France and grew up in the Pacific Northwest. She has an Art History and Studio Art degree from Scripps College and a Media and Communications master's degree from the London School of Economics. With a former career in public relations Hi, my name is Kelly Dern and I'm a designer at Sojern and illustrator by night. I occasionally write about my design process, and post pictures of what I'm working on. You can also see some of my other projects elsewhere on the web. I'm currently obsessing over color theory, CSS preprocessors and acrylic paint colors.

Interested in collaborating, have a question or just want to say hi? I would love to hear from you! Get in touch at hello@kellydern.com.

I paired the heavy and beautiful serif Galaxie Copernicus Heavy with Rooney Sans, a smooth and geometic sans serif. Both are available for web licensing.
