Vintage California poster

Illustration for Corporative typeface

I was thrilled when Monotype gave me the opportunity to create an illustration based on the typeface Corporative. Corporative is a sturdy, semi serif typeface with a little bit of a playful personality. The combination of strength and vibrance made me think of vintage travel posters. For this reason, I made an image of the Golden Gate Bridge, which I can see from my home studio.
